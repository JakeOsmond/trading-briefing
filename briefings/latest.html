News & Market Context

Demand for insurance is running 57%-74% up YoY and outpacing holiday bookings, fuelled by new seat releases (easyJet/Ryanair up sharply), entry rule changes (Spain/EU), and last month’s storms and strikes. Competitors are slashing pricing on aggregators to grab volume, driving the margin squeeze—confirmed in the latest market stats and our own "Insurance Price Scrape" (11 Mar 2026).
