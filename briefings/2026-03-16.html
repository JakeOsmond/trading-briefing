What's Driving This

Direct Single GP down Recurring

Over the last 7 days vs the same week last year, direct single GP fell about £12k to £31k, down 28%. Traffic mix worsened and conversion slipped: mobile sessions fell 14%, desktop sessions rose 19% but converted worse, desktop session-to-search fell to 13% from 15%, mobile session-to-search fell to 16% from 20%, and average GP per policy dropped to about £17 from £22; this has been running weak for six straight weeks.

  transaction_date,
  SUM(policy_count) AS policies,
  SUM(CAST(total_gross_exc_ipt_ntu_comm AS FLOAT64)) AS gp,
  SUM(CAST(total_gross_exc_ipt_ntu_comm AS FLOAT64)) / NULLIF(SUM(policy_count), 0) AS avg_gp_per_policy
FROM `hx-data-production.commercial_finance.insurance_policies_new`
WHERE distribution_channel = 'Direct'
  AND policy_type = 'Single'
  AND transaction_date BETWEEN '2026-03-08' AND '2026-03-15'
GROUP BY 1
ORDER BY 1;
