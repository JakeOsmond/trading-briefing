Trading GP slips £26k as direct single trip and mobile traffic miss the insurance search surge (week to yesterday vs LY)

At a Glance

  • Direct Single Trip GP down £6.8k — GP for direct single trip fell 15% over the last 7 days vs last year, sinking to £37.5k, nearly all margin (not volume).
  • Mobile sessions fell 22% — Mobile web traffic dropped by 7k sessions week-on-week, costing us about £16.6k in lost GP.
  • Partner Referral Single Trip GP down £5.9k — GP down 35% (£14k vs £22k) on both lower volume and worse average margin; commission up too.
  • GP from Direct Annual off £11k — Direct annual GP shrank 20% week-on-week to £45.6k (mainly volume and cost per case).
  • Worldwide policy GP slipped £12.4k — GP for worldwide destinations dropped 19% to £51.8k; shift to Europe means lower margin.

What's Driving This

Direct Single Trip GP Recurring

Direct single trip GP fell £6.8k over the last 7 days vs last year (down 15%), with margin per policy down nearly 18%; traffic was flat but average GP shrank. This is the seventh GP miss in 10 days, mainly from heavier discounting (average discount up) and higher underwriter cost, with session-to-search conversion on mobile and desktop both sharply down.
